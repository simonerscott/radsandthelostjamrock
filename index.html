Welcome to Rads and the Lost Jamrock

One fateful morning, Rads is approached by the spirit of her long deceased grandfather with a warning: Monsters have invaded the island of Jamaica! Rads must find the Lost Jamrock, a device that will be used to seal the rip in dimensions that is allowing monsters to populate the island from the underworld.

Features

Rads and the Lost Jamrock is a 3D fantasy/adventure PC game developed using the Unity 3D gaming engine. The game has thirteen distinct stages that were developed based on an iconic location in each parish in Jamaica (combining Kingston and St. Andrew). The playable character, Rads, is able walk, run, jump, roll, attack enemies and perform a polymorphic use action with varying results depending on the object being interacted with (ie collecting a sword to aid in combat or use a boat to cross a river). The player has to complete tasks for the stage he/she is presently in before proceeding to another one. A companion AI, a blue dragon, was developed to assist Rads during combat by locating her enemies and fighting them off.
